# Mesengr Charging Guide (Internal)

Internal reference for quoting. Never publish raw numbers on the site — the site
sells engagement models; you quote after the strategy call. All figures in naira,
calibrated for the Nigerian SME market (mid-2026). Adjust for inflation and your
capacity: when your pipeline is full, move every range up.

---

## 3. Recurring: care plans (monthly)

| Plan | Price | What's inside | Who it's for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Essential Care** | ₦35k – ₦60k/mo | Hosting, SSL, backups, updates, uptime monitoring, monthly report, email support | Every client, minimum. Don't hand over a site without it |
| **Growth Care** | ₦100k – ₦180k/mo | Essential + 2h content changes, performance & SEO monitoring, analytics call, priority support | Sites that are a real lead channel |
| **Growth Partner** | ₦250k – ₦500k/mo | Growth Care + monthly optimization sprint, CRO experiments, chatbot tuning, quarterly strategy, same-day SLA | Clients where the site drives revenue directly |

Annual prepay: offer 10–12 months for the price of 12 — cash flow beats margin.

## 4. Recurring: standalone SEO

- Local SEO (Google Business + on-page): **₦150k – ₦250k/mo**, 6-month minimum
- Full SEO (content + technical + links): **₦300k – ₦500k/mo**, 6-month minimum
- Never sell SEO month-to-month; it can't show results in 30 days and you'll churn.

## 5. Engagement models (what the site sells)

| Model | Typical total | Composition |
|---|---|---|
| **Launch** | ₦600k – ₦1.5m one-off | Discovery + strategy + business website + email + analytics + 30 days support. Attach Essential Care after |
| **Growth** | ₦1.8m – ₦5m one-off | Launch scope + research + ecommerce/booking/portal + chatbot + automation. Attach Growth Care |

## 6. Pricing from the Digital Assessment

The assessment emails you the lead's maturity level, budget answer and pains.
Map them like this:

| Maturity level | Lead usually needs | Open the conversation at |
|---|---|---|
| **Foundation** (score ≤ 6) | First real website, email, Google presence | Launch: ₦600k – ₦1.2m + Essential Care ₦40k/mo |
| **Growth** (score 7–11) | Rebuild/upgrade, bookings, chatbot, SEO | Growth: ₦1.8m – ₦3.5m + Growth Care ₦120k/mo |
| **Scale** (score ≥ 12) | Optimization, automation, AI, consulting | Growth Partner ₦300k+/mo or Partner retainer |

Cross-check against their budget answer:
- Budget **below** the level's floor → descope to a phased plan (phase 1 now,
  phase 2 in 90 days) rather than discounting. Same total, spread out.
- Budget **above** → don't leave it on the table; add the chatbot/automation line.

Pains → line items: "enquiries unanswered" → chatbot; "admin work" → automation;
"invisible on Google" → SEO retainer; "no idea what the site does" → analytics +
Growth Care.

## 7. Quoting rules

1. **Anchor high, itemize down.** Present Partner/Growth first, then Launch feels accessible.
2. **50% deposit** to book the slot, 40% at design approval, 10% at launch. Web apps: monthly milestones.
3. **Rush fee**: +25–40% for "yesterday" timelines (the assessment tells you who they are).
4. **Third-party costs are pass-through, never absorbed**: domain (~₦25k–₦40k/yr), premium plugins/APIs, ad spend, Resend/hosting overages. List them separately on every quote.
5. **Every build quote includes 12 months of a care plan line** — pre-ticked. Removing it is the client's active choice.
6. **Free strategy call ≠ free consulting.** One call. If they want a written strategy without a build, that's the paid audit (₦100k–₦250k).
7. **Annual review**: raise care plan prices ~10–15% yearly for new clients; grandfather existing ones a year behind.
8. **Never discount, re-scope.** A smaller price gets a smaller scope — protects your positioning and future pricing.

## 8. Floor prices (walk-away numbers)

- No website project below **₦250k**
- No monthly relationship below **₦30k/mo**
- No custom app below **₦2.5m**
- No SEO commitment shorter than **6 months**

If a lead can't meet the floor, point them at the blog and free tools and stay
warm — Foundation businesses become Growth clients in 12–18 months.
